Password Security

A good password is long and has letters, punctuation marks, symbols, and numbers.

Tips for creating a secure password
  • Mix capital and lowercase letters
  • Include numbers
  • Add symbols or punctuation marks
Additional tips
  • Don't reuse passwords for multiple important accounts, such as your email account and online banking.
  • Don't use a password that contains personal information (name, birth date, etc.)
  • Don't use words that can be found in a dictionary
